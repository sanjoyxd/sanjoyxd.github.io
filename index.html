Let me introduce myself

I am a 28 years old guy from Barpeta Road, Assam. Currently working as a Computer teacher in Don Bosco School in my area. Previously worked at multiple IT Companies in Bangalore and Delhi.

Along with teaching I do carry interest in Sketching and Photography. My school salary does not fullfill the requirnments here for my family so I do provide tuition class from class 8 to class 12 on Computer subject. And also Sketch other peoples face by Pencil and Charcoal if I get paid enough.

Services

Realistic Sketch

The sketch will be very much similar to a printed black & white Photo. Mostly graphite pencils will be used on an ivory smooth paper.

Artistic Sketch

The sketch will be portray of the person but not exactly same as the captured photo. Mostly charcoal pencils will be used on a rough paper.

Photography

Photography is a creative way to know yourself. Patience and dedication makes them perfect. This service is not available to save time.

Tutuion Classes

Only for Computer Subject for class 8 to class 12. Not more than 4 student in a batch. Class for pencil sketch is available with special request.
